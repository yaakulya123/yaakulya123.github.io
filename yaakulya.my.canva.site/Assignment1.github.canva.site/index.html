Swimming Pool

"The NYUAD Swimming Pool is a hidden oasis of tranquility nestled within the bustling campus of New York University Abu Dhabi. As you step into this aquatic haven, the scent of chlorinated water fills the air, inviting you to immerse yourself in its crystal-clear depths." - Review by an Anonymous Student.


This remarkable swimming pool is not just a place for leisure and exercise; it is a space where students and faculty come together to unwind, rejuvenate, and build a sense of community. The glistening azure waters beckon swimmers of all skill levels, offering a refreshing respite from the demands of academic life.

Library

"The NYUAD Library stands as an architectural marvel, nestled within the vibrant campus of New York University Abu Dhabi. As you step inside, you are greeted by an atmosphere that exudes intellectual curiosity and a thirst for knowledge. The library's soaring ceilings and expansive windows allow natural light to cascade onto its meticulously curated shelves, housing a treasure trove of literature from around the world." - Review by an Anonymous Student.


What sets the NYUAD Library apart is not just its impressive collection but also its commitment to fostering a community of scholars and thinkers. It serves as a sanctuary where students and faculty come together to engage in intellectual discourse, exchange ideas, and embark on a journey of self-discovery.

Market Place

"The Market Place (Food Cafe) at NYUAD is a culinary haven where flavors from around the globe unite, creating a vibrant and enticing dining experience. Step into this bustling marketplace of gastronomic delights, and let your senses embark on a captivating journey." Review by an Anonymous Student


As you enter, the air is filled with tantalizing aromas that waft from various food stations, each offering a unique culinary adventure. The Market Place is a melting pot of diverse cuisines, where international flavors intermingle harmoniously. From fragrant spices of the Middle East to the bold spices of Asia and the comforting familiarity of Western classics, there is something to satiate every craving.
